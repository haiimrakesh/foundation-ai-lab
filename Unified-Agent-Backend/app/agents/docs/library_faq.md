# Library Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I become a member?
Fill out the membership form online or at the front desk. Provide proof of residence and a valid ID.

### Can I reserve books?
Yes, reservations can be made online. Reserved books must be collected within 48 hours.

### What happens if I lose a book?
You must pay the replacement cost plus a ₹200 processing fee.

### Are there study rooms?
Yes, study rooms can be booked for 2-hour slots. Group study rooms accommodate up to 6 people.

### Does the library host events?
Yes, monthly book clubs, author talks, and children’s story hours are organized.
