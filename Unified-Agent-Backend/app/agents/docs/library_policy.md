# Library Policy and Guidelines

## Borrowing Rules
- Each member may borrow up to **2 books** at a time.
- Loan period is **14 days** per book.
- Renewals are allowed once, provided the book is not reserved by another member.
- Late fee: ₹10 per day per book.

## Membership
- Membership is open to all residents of the city.
- Students receive a 50% discount on membership fees.
- Corporate memberships allow up to 20 employees to share access.

## Digital Resources
- Members can access e-books and audiobooks via the library portal.
- Digital loans expire automatically after 14 days.
- Printing from digital resources is limited to 10 pages per session.

## Conduct
- Silence must be maintained in reading areas.
- Food and drinks are not allowed inside the library.
- Mobile phones must be on silent mode.

## Special Collections
- Rare manuscripts are available only for supervised viewing.
- Archives can be accessed by researchers with prior approval.
