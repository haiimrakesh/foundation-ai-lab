# Book Summaries

## The Great Gatsby
A novel by F. Scott Fitzgerald about wealth, love, and the American Dream in 1920s America.  
Themes: illusion vs reality, class divide, pursuit of happiness.

## War and Peace
A novel by Leo Tolstoy exploring history, philosophy, and human struggle during the Napoleonic wars.  
Themes: fate vs free will, love, war, peace.

## Pride and Prejudice
A novel by Jane Austen about manners, marriage, and morality in 19th century England.  
Themes: social class, pride, prejudice, love.

## 1984
A dystopian novel by George Orwell depicting a totalitarian regime that controls thought and behavior.  
Themes: surveillance, freedom, truth, oppression.

## To Kill a Mockingbird
A novel by Harper Lee about racial injustice and moral growth in the American South.  
Themes: justice, empathy, childhood innocence.
